About me


I’m a hardworking individual with a curious mind, passionate about mastering the art of web and app development. Currently, I’m pursuing my education at triOS College, with the goal of becoming a skilled and proficient web and app developer. My journey is just beginning, and I’m eager to continue learning and growing in this exciting field. What sets me apart is my flexibility and adaptability, demonstrated by my diverse career path. I started my professional journey working in the hospitality industry for 16 years. In 2015, after moving to Canada, I transitioned to a maintenance role and later challenged myself as a Maintenance Coordinator, where I successfully supervised and controlled construction projects with budgets exceeding $1 million. Now, I’ve embraced a new challenge—becoming a web developer. My varied experiences have taught me resilience, problem-solving, and the ability to learn and excel in completely different fields. These qualities fuel my passion for this field and help me approach development with creativity and determination. I’m grateful for every opportunity to learn, and I value constructive feedback and support as I embark on this journey of growth, innovation, and exploration.
